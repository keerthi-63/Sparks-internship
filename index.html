ABOUT US

The trust began with an abandoned baby and so they are in a way the foundation of the organization. There are homes and schools where they are nurtured and educated to find a career and lead a dignified life.

The wandering mentally ill, however bizarre they may seem, are human beings like you and me. Our team rescues them, gives them psychiatric treatment and reunites them with their family

SERVICES

The fund which is collected is used to enroll the children in school. Apart these children first generation students from economically backward families and children of migrant workers and those with special needs, all receive the type of education suitable for them.

All the activities of Hindustan Trust are an attempt to respond to human suffering : The hands of compassion cross all boundaries to reach out to outcasts of society.
